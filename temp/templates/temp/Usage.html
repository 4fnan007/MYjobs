How To ​​Use Our Service / MyJob

Guide on how to navigate, utilize, and optimize MyJob platform from registration to advanced features, step-by-step instructions ensure seamless integration into your workflow,

01

For Service Providers:

  • To sign up as a service provider, navigate to the homepage and select the "Service" option.
  • Complete the registration process by providing essential details, including company information and a government-approved certificate or ID.
  • Await admin approval, which typically takes less than 24 hours.
  • Once approved, log in using the provided email and password.
  • Post job listings relevant to your service offerings.
  • Review and manage worker applications, including verification and acceptance or rejection.
  • Manage your profile, job postings, worker applications, payments, messages, payment history, complaints, and inquiries.
02

For Workers:

  • Choose the "Worker" option on the homepage to register.
  • Complete your registration by providing necessary personal information.
  • Wait for admin approval, which is usually confirmed within 24 hours.
  • Sign in using your registered email and password upon approval.
  • Explore available job listings matching your skills and preferences.
  • Apply to job listings by submitting your name, location, phone number, and optionally, your resume.
  • Monitor the status of your applications, payment details, messages, and work history.
  • File complaints and review responses from service providers.
03

If login is unsuccessful, there are several potential reasons:

  • Incorrect password or email: If you've mistyped your password or email address, you won't able to log in.
  • Existing email or phone number: If the email or phone number you're trying to use for registration already exists in our system, you won't be able to sign up again.
  • Pending admin approval: If your registration form is awaiting approval from the admin, you won't be able to log in until your registration is approved.
